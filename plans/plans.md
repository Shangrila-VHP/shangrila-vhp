# DIY Shelter Plans

## Overview
Welcome to the DIY Shelter Plans repository! This collection aims to empower individuals to create safe, comfortable shelters within their budget constraints. **Shangrila-VHP** provides the expertise, resources, and vetting to ensure structures are safe and resilient.

**Important:** Always consult with professional architects and engineers before building any structure you intend to inhabit.

### Purpose
- Provide accessible, vetted shelter building plans
- Enable cost-effective housing solutions for diverse needs
- Empower self-sufficiency in housing construction
- Share construction methods that meet safety standards

### Contents
The plans are organized by complexity:

1. **Basic Shelters**
   - Simple, minimalist designs
   - Minimal material requirements
   - Quick construction timelines (days/weeks)

2. **Intermediate Projects**
   - Enhanced comfort and functionality
   - Modular additions for expansion
   - Weather-resistant considerations

3. **Advanced Builds**
   - Sustainable, off-grid options
   - Long-term, permanent solutions
   - Complex architectural structures

### Using These Plans
Each plan includes comprehensive resources:

- **Material lists** with quantities and specifications
- **Cost estimates** (materials, tools, labor)
- **Step-by-step construction guides**
- **Safety guidelines** and warning labels
- **Required tools** and skill level assessment
- **Time estimates** for each construction phase

### Important Notes
- **Legal**: Always check local building codes and zoning regulations
- **Practical**: Verify material availability in your region
- **Environmental**: Consider climate-specific adaptations
- **Realistic**: Honestly assess your skill level and available time
- **Safety first**: Never compromise on structural integrity or safety features

## Self-Sustaining Economic Models
*Main Architect: @genidma (Adeel Khan)*  
*Facilitator: Antigravity*

The Architecture of Abundance encompasses both physical structures and the circular economic systems that sustain them.

### 💎 Long Service Good Behavior (LSGB) Investment Fund
A revolutionary circular economic model that pays off shelter capital costs and funds future expansion through veteran-led labor and strategic investment.

#### Mathematical Engine
- **Participation**: 50% of sheltered residents engage in meaningful part-time work
- **Contribution**: 10% voluntary contribution from gross income (based on $20/hr @ 20hrs/week)
- **Annual Velocity**: Target ~$14.5M/year for the fund (7,000 veteran pilot projection)
- **Payback Loop**: Shelters (base cost ~$5,000) paid off in ~2.5 years, creating a debt-free community

#### Operational Roadmap
1. **Governance**: Establish the LSGB Foundation (non-profit trust/endowments)
2. **Pilot Integration**: Align with "Dignity Center" roles and responsibilities
3. **Multiplier Effect**: Reinvest profits into rental prototypes (Parks Canada/Airbnb model) for passive income

### 🍲 Galaxy Soups & Dignity Centers
Integrating automated nutrition with essential hygiene to restore hope and dignity.

#### Implementation Pillars
- **Automation**: High-hygiene, scalable soup production using collaborative robots
- **Dignity Pods**: Modular 3D-printable units for showers, laundry, and grooming
- **Economics**: "Pay What You Can" model (inspired by JBJ Soul Kitchen)
- **Synergy**: Culinary volunteer programs + professional support services

### 🛡️ Hero's Haven (Housing for Injured Front-Line Workers)
A global safety net for injured front-line workers (medical, fire, police, military) and their caregivers, emphasizing resident ownership and skills transition.

#### Core Pillars
- **Resident Equity**: Properties owned by residents, capturing appreciation tied to LSGB Fund growth
- **Empowerment Engine**: Transition to technical fields ([Issue #65](https://github.com/Shangrila-VHP/shangrila-vhp/issues/65)) and certified investor roles
- **Social Infrastructure**: Integrated support with mandatory weekly check-ups
- **Financial Shield**: Specialized insurance models (leveraging [IEEE broker concepts](https://www.ieee.org/membership/discounts))
