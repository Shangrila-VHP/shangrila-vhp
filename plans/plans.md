# DIY Shelter Plans

## Overview
- This is the DIY shelter plans repository. This collection aims to empower individuals to create safe, comfortable shelters within their budget constraints.
- Shangrila-VHP will do the heavy lifting and ensure that the structures are safe and resilient. But it's always crucial to consult with professional architects and engineers before anyone decides to build a shelter or an abode that they are going to be staying in.

### Purpose
- Provide accessible shelter building plans
- Enable cost-effective housing solutions
- Empower self-sufficiency in housing
- Share vetted construction methods

### Contents
1. Basic Shelters
   - Simple designs
   - Minimal material requirements
   - Quick construction timelines

2. Intermediate Projects
   - Enhanced features
   - Modular additions
   - Weather considerations

3. Advanced Builds
   - Sustainable options
   - Long-term solutions
   - Complex structures

### Using These Plans
- Each plan includes:
  - Material lists
  - Cost estimates
  - Construction steps
  - Safety guidelines
  - Required tools
  - Time estimates

### Important Notes
- Always check local building codes
- Verify material availability
- Consider climate requirements
- Assess skill level needed
- Review safety precautions
- Never ever compromise safety

The Architecture of Abundance is not just about physical structures, but the circular economic systems that sustain them.

### üíé Long Service Good Behavior (LSGB) Investment Fund
A circular economic model designed to pay off shelter capital costs and fund future expansion through veteran-led labor and strategic investment.

#### Mathematical Engine
- **Participation**: 50% of sheltered residents engaging in meaningful part-time work.
- **Contribution**: 10% voluntary contribution from gross income ($20/hr @ 20hrs/week).
- **Annual Velocity**: Target of ~$14.5M/year for the fund (based on a 7,000 veteran pilot).
- **Payback Loop**: Shelters (base cost ~$5,000) can be fully paid off in ~2.5 years, creating a debt-free community.

#### Operational Roadmap
1. **Governance**: Establishment of the LSGB Foundation (non-profit trust/endowments).
2. **Pilot Integration**: Mandatory alignment with "Dignity Center" roles.
3. **Multiplier Effect**: Reinvesting profits into rental prototypes (e.g., Parks Canada/Airbnb) to generate passive donor income.

Integrating automated nutrition with essential hygiene and harm reduction to restore hope and dignity.

#### Implementation Pillars
- **Automation**: High-hygiene, high-scale soup production using co-bot (collaborative robot) designs.
- **Dignity Pods**: Modular 3D-printable units for showers, laundry, and grooming.
- **Economics**: "Pay What you Can" model inspired by the success of JBJ Soul Kitchen.
- **Synergy**: Culinary student volunteer tracks and professional mental health/social work support.
