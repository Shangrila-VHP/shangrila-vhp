Our Mission

A World of Hope & Healing

The Vision

Originally imagined to be a veterans healing place, project has evolved to help provide safe/affordable/eco-friendly housing to as many humans as possible. We aim to plummet the cost of quality housing to $1000 or less.

The Problem

One veteran or first-responder experiencing homelessness is too many. Supply must be increased in a manner that is safe, secure, and at a price point that is not an excessive burden on the taxpayers.
